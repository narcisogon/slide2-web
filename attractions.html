Our Attractions

Sunny Splash Waterpark offers a wide range of attractions designed to provide fun and excitement for all ages. Dive into adventure with our thrilling water slides, enjoy the waves in our expansive wave pool, or relax along the serene lazy river.

Thrilling Water Slides

Our water slides are engineered for maximum excitement and safety. Featuring multiple levels and twists, they offer a thrilling ride for both kids and adults.

  • Multiple slide options
  • High-speed thrills
  • Safe and secure

Wave Pool

Experience the fun of ocean waves in our wave pool. Perfect for swimming, floating, and enjoying time with family and friends.

  • Realistic wave simulations
  • Safe for all ages
  • Lifeguards on duty

Lazy River

Relax and unwind as you drift along our lazy river. It's the perfect way to enjoy a peaceful ride while soaking up the sun.

  • Gentle currents
  • Scenic surroundings
  • Comfortable float devices available
