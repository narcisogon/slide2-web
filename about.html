About Us

Welcome to Sunny Splash Waterpark, your ultimate destination for fun and adventure! Established in 2010, we have been providing thrilling water attractions, relaxing pools, and unforgettable experiences for families and thrill-seekers alike.

Our mission is to create a safe and enjoyable environment where everyone can have a splash-tastic time. From our state-of-the-art water slides to our serene lazy river, we offer something for everyone.

At Sunny Splash, we believe in continuous improvement and innovation. Our team is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of safety and customer satisfaction, ensuring that every visit is memorable.

Our Journey

2010 - Grand Opening

Sunny Splash Waterpark officially opened its doors to the public, offering a variety of water attractions.

2012 - Wave Pool Launch

Introduced our state-of-the-art wave pool, providing endless fun for all ages.

2015 - Lazy River Addition

Added a relaxing lazy river for guests seeking a more laid-back experience.

2020 - Awarded Best Waterpark

Received the prestigious "Best Waterpark" award for excellence in customer service and attractions.
